    About me

    Welcome to a non-linear journey inside my corner of the internet. A world where nothing is appropriate and everything is not as it should be. ＼(＾▽＾)／

    I am a tech geek with a passion for the retro art style. With a keen eye for aesthetics, I tried to accomplish a pixel perfect layout alongside clean, maintainable and reusable code using JavaScript(),{CSS} and <HTML>.

    I made this website with the goal to do something that felt like me, something super flawed and clumsy and kind of thoughtless, something that like me needed music to survive. That's why my website has a very teenage angst sountrack.
    (＾▽＾)

    In my spare time I enjoy reparing thing as much as breaking them, anything nature related and going to the flee market to find all kinds of lost pearls (I have so many walkmans and casettes it's starting to be a problem). As you could have imagined I have a strong enthusiasm for everything that is technology releated (IT, mechanical, material, electronics etc).

    And I may or may not have a thing for the 80's...
